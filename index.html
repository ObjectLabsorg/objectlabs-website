About Object Labs

Founded by a mechanical engineer with 15 years of industry experience, Object Labs is committed to making STEM education accessible and engaging for the next generation of innovators.

Our unique approach combines hands-on learning with modern content creation, allowing us to reach and inspire students both locally and globally.

Based in Zionsville, Indiana, we offer in-person STEM programs while creating educational content that reaches audiences worldwide.
